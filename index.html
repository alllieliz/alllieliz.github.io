Who Am I

That's the question we all ask at least once in our lives, right? "Who am I?" Even though I've never met you, you're probably someone amazing.

But who am I? I'm a recent graduate working at a design studio in North Carolina. I'm also an Aquarius and a 4w3. I'm taking this Khan Academy course because I want to learn more about HTML and CSS - and I'm broke. So shoutout to Khan Academy for this free resource.

I have a cute boyfriend of three (almost four) years, a cat named Boo that I found on the side of the highway, a big collection of books, a pretty nice island on Animal Crossing, anxiety, and light brown hair I have a hard time managing.

This is a pretty short post, but I don't want to bore you. Thanks for reading! Keep going if you want to learn a bit more beyond the basics.
